is the process of creating software applications that run on various devices, includ ing smartphones, tablets, and computers. In today's digital landscape, app development is crucial for businesses aiming to enhance user engagement and streamline operations. The development process typically involves several key stages: planning, where developers identify the target audience and define the app's purpose; design, which includes creating wireframes and user interface (UI) designs; development, where the actual coding takes place; testing, to ensure the app is functional and free of bugs; deployment, which involves launching the app on app stores; and maintenance, where updates and bug fixes are implemented post-launch. .
